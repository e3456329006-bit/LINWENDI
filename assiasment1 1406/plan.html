Goal

Raise awareness, promote dental services effectively, and encourage both new and returning patients to choose 100SMILES Dental Care over competitors. Highlight “friendly, reliable, and compassionate care”, 50 + years of experience, and a full range of services — from check-ups and cleanings to implants and orthodontics — while driving bookings through visible promotions and strong calls-to-action.

Success Evaluation

Awareness: growth in website traffic and engagement time. Promotion: clicks to service pages and uptake of promotional offers. Conversion & Retention: increase in new bookings via email discounts and repeat visits for annual check-ups.

Target Audience

Families and individuals of all ages — from children to retirees — who value preventive care and long-term oral health. Also includes patients seeking specialized or emergency treatment, price-sensitive first-time visitors, and users searching for credible oral-health advice online.
